Instant Pot Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Servings: Calories: 650kcal author: diana Source: Littlesunnykitchen.com

Ingredients

1 large chicken breast diced

4 cups (10 oz) (300 grams) tagliatelle or fettuccine pasta

2 cups (150 grams) chestnut mushrooms sliced

2 cloves garlic minced

1 tsp thyme

1 tsp rosemary

1/4 tsp ground nutmeg

1/4 tsp ground black pepper

2 cups (500 ml) chicken or vegetable stock

1/2 tsp salt

5 oz (150 grams) cream cheese

1 cup (200 grams) mozzarella shredded

Directions

Set your Instant Pot on “Saute” for 5 minutes, and heat some oil at the pot. Add the diced chicken cubes, and lightly brown them. The chicken doesn’t need to be cooked through, just slightly browned.

If using mushrooms, then add sliced mushrooms, minced garlic, and herbs. I normally use rosemary and thyme but feel free any other herbs that you like.

Add your pasta to the pot, I normally use fettuccine, tagliatelle or linguine. Then add your chicken or vegetable stock. Gently press down the pasta to make sure that it’s fully covered in the liquid (otherwise it won’t be cooked through). You could also add some ground black pepper at this stage.

Cover with the lid, seal the valve. Cook on manual “high pressure” for 5 minutes. Once the cooking is finished, do a quick release and open the lid.

Stir in the cream cheese, shredded mozzarella, and salt. Keep stirring until you get a creamy consistency. Have a taste and see if you need to add more salt or ground black pepper and serve!
