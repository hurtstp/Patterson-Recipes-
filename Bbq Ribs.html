Bbq Ribs

★★★★★

Pork

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Yield: 6-8 servings Source: Damndelicious.net

Ingredients

1 tablespoon brown sugar

2 teaspoons kosher salt

2 teaspoons onion powder

2 teaspoons chili powder

2 teaspoons ground mustard

1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika

1 teaspoon dried thyme

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

3/4 teaspoon ground cumin

1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

2 (2-pound) racks baby back pork ribs, trimmed of excess fat, rinsed, and patted dry

barbeque sauce

Directions

In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, salt, onion powder, chili powder, mustard, paprika, thyme, oregano, garlic powder, black pepper, cumin and cayenne pepper.

Remove the membrane from the underside of the ribs, pulling it down the entire length.

Preheat oven to 275 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil.

Place ribs, meat side up, on baking sheets. Rub spice mixture over both sides of ribs. Wrap with foil to make a packet.

Place into oven and bake 2 1/2 to 3 hours. Unwrap and spread BBQ sauce on ribs. Place back in oven and cook until BBQ sauce starts to bubble.

Serve immediately with remaining barbecue sauce.
