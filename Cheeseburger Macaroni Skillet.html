Cheeseburger Macaroni Skillet

★★★★★

Beef, Pasta

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: prep/total time: 30 min. Servings: 6 servings Source: Tasteofhome.com

Ingredients

1 pound lean ground beef (90% lean)

1 pound uncooked elbow rotini or cavatappi pasta

2 1/2 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth

1 15oz can tomato sauce

1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce

1 tablespoon mustard powder

1 cup chopped onion

6 oz deli cheese (Muenster or provolone, chopped)

1/2 cup dill pickles, chopped

Directions

In a large skillet, cook beef and half of the onions over medium heat 6-8 minutes or until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles; drain.

Stir in macaroni, broth, worchestershire sauce, tomato sauce and mustard; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, 10-15 minutes or until macaroni is tender. Stir in cheese until melted. Sprinkle with pickles and remaining onions.
