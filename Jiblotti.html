Jiblotti

Desserts

Ingredients

For Dough


1/2 cup shortening

4 cups flour

1 cup sugar

3 eggs

3/4 cups milk

1 tsp cream of tartar

1 tsp baking soda

zest of one lemon


Filling


1 Large Hershey bar

1 lb. almonds

1 bottle maraschino cherries (save liquid)

1 lb. dates


Glaze


confectioners sugars

Liquid from cherries


Description

Christmas recipe from Mom. This is the original recipe.

Directions

Mix shortening, sugar, zest and eggs. Add half of the flour, then milk and then rest of flour, cream of tartar and baking soda. Mix together well. Chill over night.

Roll out dough. Add fillings and roll up like a jelly roll. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Cool completely.

Drizzle glaze over top.
