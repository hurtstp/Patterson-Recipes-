Roasted Chicken with Balsamic Vinaigrette

Chicken

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 1 hr Difficulty: Easy Servings: Yield: 6 servings Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

1/4 cup balsamic vinegar

2 tablespoons Dijon mustard

zest of one lemon

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

2 garlic cloves, chopped

2 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and freshly ground black pepper

6-8 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin on

Directions

Whisk the vinegar, mustard, lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper in small bowl to blend. Combine the vinaigrette and chicken pieces in a large resealable plastic bag; seal the bag and toss to coat. Refrigerate, turning the chicken pieces occasionally, for at least 2 hours and up to 1 day.

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Remove chicken from the bag and arrange the chicken pieces on a large baking dish. Roast until the chicken is just cooked through, about 45min to 1 hour. If your chicken browns too quickly, cover it with foil for the remaining cooking time. Transfer the chicken to a serving platter.
