Peanut Butter Cups

★★★★★

Desserts

Source: Gramma's Recipe

Ingredients

1 cup smooth peanut butter

1/4 cup butter, room temperature

1 teaspoon vanilla

1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar

1/2 cup flour

6 ounces chocolate chips

Directions

Mix peanut butter, butter and vanilla together until smooth. Gradually add sugar and flour. It will be thick and creamy. Should hold it's shape when you form it into balls. Place balls into mini paper cupcake cups.

Melt chocolate chips. Using a spoon, drip chocolate on top of the balls. Just enough to cover the top. Let chocolate set at room temperature and then store in refrigerator.
