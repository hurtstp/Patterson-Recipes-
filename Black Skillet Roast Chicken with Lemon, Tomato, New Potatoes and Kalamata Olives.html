Black Skillet Roast Chicken with Lemon, Tomato, New Potatoes and Kalamata Olives

Chicken

Ingredients

4 TABLESPOONS OF EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL, DIVIDED

1 LARGE LEMON, THINLY SLICED

6 LARGE CLOVES OF GARLIC, CHOPPED, 3 TABLESPOONS

1/4 TEASPOON SALT

FRESHLY GROUND BLACK PEPPER TO TASTE

JUICE AND ZEST OF 1/2 LEMON

8 BONELESS CHICKEN THIGHS

1 POUND RED NEW POTATOES, SCRUBBED AND QUARTERED

10 CHERRY TOMATOES

15 KALMATA OLIVES

1 TEASPOON CHOPPED FRESH ROSEMARY PLUS A SPRING OF FRESH ROSEMARY


Directions

Preheat over to 450F

Wipe a 10 inch cast-iron over proof skillet with olive oil, and then overlap the lemon slices on the bottom.

In a medium bowl, stir together 2 teaspoons of the chopped garlic with 1/4 teaspoon salt, pepper, lemon juice and zest, and 1 tablespoon of the olive oil. Toss the chicken pieces in this mixture, then arrange in one layer over the lemon slices. Top with any garlic mixture left in the bowl.

In the same bowl, stir together the remaining teaspoon of garlic and remaining olive oil. Add the potatoes, tomatoes, olives, and fresh rosemary leaves. Season generously with salt and pepper and toss to mix. Arrange the vegetables around the chicken and lay the rosemary spring over the top.

Roast, uncovered for 60 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted into the chicken reads 175 F. Serve immediately from the skillet, spooning any pan juices over the meat and vegetables.
