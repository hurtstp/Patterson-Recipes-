The Ultimate Egg Slut Breakfast Sandwich

★★★★★

Pork, Sandwiches & Quesadilas & Burgers & Pizza

Prep Time: 25 minutes Cook Time: 25 minutes Servings: Servings: 2 Source: Vice.com

Ingredients

for the Sriracha mayo:

1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce

2 tablespoons mayonnaise (I use Hellman's)

for the marbleized eggs:

4 tablespoons butter

8 free-range eggs

salt, to taste

4 tablespoons sliced chives

for the pork:

1 can Spam

to assemble:

1 handful sliced scallions

1 pack King's Hawaiian dinner rolls

Directions

1. For the Sriracha mayo: Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix until blended.

3. If you're using Spam, slice the Spam in 1/4-inch slices and sear on medium-high heat until lightly browned.

4. To assemble the sandwiches, slice the rolls in half (I use 4 rolls still stuck together) and toast each, cut-side down. Slather the Sriracha mayo on the toasted side both rolls. Layer Spam or pork chop on the bottom bun. Layer fried egg on top of the meat. Top the eggs with sliced scallions. Lid the sandwich with the top bun and enjoy.
