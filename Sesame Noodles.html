Sesame Noodles

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: Yields: 8 Source: Thepioneerwoman.com

Ingredients

12 oz. thin noodles, cooked and drained

1/4 c. soy sauce

2 tbsp. sugar

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp. rice vinegar

3 tbsp. pure sesame oil

1/2 tsp. hot chili oil

4 tbsp. canola oil

4 whole green onions, sliced thin

Directions

Whisk all ingredients (except noodles and green onion) together in a bowl. Taste and adjust ingredients as needed.

Pour sauce over warm noodles and toss to coat.

Sprinkle with green onion and toss.

Notes

load them with red bell pepper, fresh cilantro, or cooked, chopped shrimp.
