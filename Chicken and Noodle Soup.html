Chicken and Noodle Soup

★★★★★

Chicken, Pasta, Soup & Chili & Stews

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 15 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Yield: 2 quarts of soup Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

2 tablespoons (2 turns around the pan) extra-virgin olive oil

4 medium carrots, peeled and chopped

1 medium onion, chopped

3 ribs celery, chopped

1 tsp dried thyme leaves

2 bay leaves, fresh or dried

Salt and pepper

6 to 8 cups good quality chicken stock

2 pounds (the average weight of 1 package) chicken breast tenders, diced

bag of wide egg noodles

Directions

Place a large pot over moderate heat and add extra-virgin olive oil. Work close to the stove and add vegetables to the pot as you chop, in the order they are listed.

Add thyme and bay leaves and season vegetables with salt and pepper, to taste. Add stock to the pot and raise flame to bring liquid to a boil. Add diced chicken tenderloins, return soup to a boil, and reduce heat back to moderate. Cook chicken 2 minutes and add noodles. Add chicken stock to cover all the noodles. Cook soup an additional 6 minutes or until noodles are tender and remove soup from the heat.

Remove bay leaves and serve. This is a thick soup. Add up to 2 cups of water if you like chicken soup with lots of broth.
