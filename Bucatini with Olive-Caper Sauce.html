Bucatini with Olive-Caper Sauce

★★★★★

Pasta

Prep Time: 15 min Cook Time: 10 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Yield: 4 servings Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

Kosher salt

12 ounces bucatini or spaghetti

1 small clove garlic

2 tablespoons capers, drained and rinsed

Pinch of red pepper flakes

2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

1 pound tomatoes, diced or 1 can diced tomatoes

2/3 cup pitted kalamata or nicoise olives, chopped

3 tablespoons unsalted butter

3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil or 1 tablespoon basil in a tube

8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, finely chopped

Directions

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook as the label directs. Reserve 1/2 cup cooking water, then drain the pasta.

Meanwhile, mince the garlic, 1 tablespoon capers and the red pepper flakes. Cook the tomatoes in the olive oil and butter. Added the capers, garlic, red pepper flakes and olives once the tomatoes were soft. Add the pasta right from the boiling water to tomato mix. Mix and add about a cup of the pasta water. Stir in the basil and cheese. Season with salt if needed.
