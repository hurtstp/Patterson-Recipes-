Creamy Ravioli Soup

Pasta, Pork, Soup & Chili & Stews

Source: Alilyloveaffair.com

Ingredients

1/2 ground Italian Sausage

1 can roasted red tomatoes

2 tablespoons of olive oil

1 medium onion, chopped

1 garlic clove, minced

4 cups chicken stock

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

Pinch of salt, to taste

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon dried basil

Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)

1 bag of fresh spinach

1 cup half and half or whole milk

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Shredded Parmesan cheese, garnish

2 10 ounce packages of ravioli

Directions

Add in olive oil to a large pot. Heat pot and add ground Italian sausage and saute until slightly brown. Be sure to stir the sausage regularly to ensure it does not stick to the bottom of the pan.

When the sausage is cooked about 50% of the way, add in chopped onions and garlic and continue to saute until onions are softened.

Add chicken broth, roasted red tomatoes, basil, Italian seasoning and salt and pepper.

Bring to a boil and stir in half and half, ravioli, grated Parmesan cheese and kale or spinach. Continue to cook until the spinach is soft or slightly wilted and pasta is softened slightly, about 5 minutes.

Add any additional seasoning to taste.

Serve and enjoy!!

Top with Parmesan cheese, too!
