Summer Chicken Salad

★★★★★

Salad

Ingredients

3 cups cooked chicken, chopped to bite size pieces

1 red, yellow or orange pepper (optional - Sam doesn't like it...)

1 English cucumber, remove seeds and chop into bit size pieces

1 avocado

1 bag frozen corn, defrosted or 2 small cans of summer crisp corn, drained and rinsed

1 pint grape tomatoes, cut in half

Mozzarella Cheese balls


Dressing:

basil (if you have fresh, tear into small pieces. If you are using basil in a tube, use about 1 tablespoon)

3/4 cup olive oil

1/4 cup balsamic vinegar

garlic clove, grated or about 1 teaspoon garlic powder.

salt and pepper to taste

Directions

Add all salad ingredients to a large bowl. Make dressing in a measuring cup then add to salad. Taste and add salt if needed.
