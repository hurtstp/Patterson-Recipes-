Baked Potatoes with Meatballs

Ingredients

For the Baked Potatoes:

3 - 6 large Russet Potatoes

1 1/2 tablespoon Olive Oil

1 tsp Salt


For Meatballs:

1 1/2 pounds ground beef

1 small onion (coarsely grated, lightly squeezed)

2 cloves garlic (minced)

1 egg

3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs

2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

1/2 tbsp tomato paste

1 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1 tsp paprika

1/4 cup fresh parsley (optional)

Oregano (optional)


For Sauce:

4 tbsp olive oil

1 med onion (diced)

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 1/2 cups tomato sauce

1 1/4 cup water

salt and pepper to taste


For Assembly:

4-6 tbsp butter

1 cup shredded mozzarella

salt and pepper to taste

grated parmesan cheese

Directions

Prepare the potatoes: Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Drizzle potatoes with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, rubbing to ensure the entire surface is covered. Poke holes into potatoes before wrapping them in foil. Place in oven on middle rack jand bake until tender, about 45 minutes.
