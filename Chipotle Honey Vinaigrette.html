Chipotle Honey Vinaigrette

Seasonings, Sauces and Spices

Prep Time: 10 mins Servings: Serves 8 Source: Loveandlemons.com

Ingredients

½ cup avocado oil

¼ cup fresh lime juice

1 chipotle pepper from a can of chipotles in adobo

2 tablespoons honey

1 garlic clove

½ teaspoon cumin

½ teaspoon sea salt

Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

In a small food processor, combine the oil, lime juice, chipotle, honey, garlic, cumin, salt, and several grinds of pepper. Pulse until combined.
