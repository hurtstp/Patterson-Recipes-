Better than Basic Corn Muffins

★★★★★

Bread, Rolls & Muffins

Ingredients

3/4 Cup (1 1/2 sticks) unsalted butter, melted


1 cup flour


3/4 cup cornmeal


3/4 cup sugar


2 teaspoon baking powder


1/3 teaspoon salt


2 large eggs


1/2 cup milk, room temp


1/2 teaspoon vanilla


Directions

Whisk together dry ingredients.

Whisk together eggs, milk and vanilla. Slowly add melted butter to milk mixture.

Fold in wet ingredients to dry until just combined.

Spoon into paper cup lined muffin tins.

Bake at 375 degrees for 15 - 20 minutes until golden brown on top.

Notes

Blueberry muffins- 2 cups flour, no cornmeal and add 1 1/2 cup blueberries at the end. Chocolate chip - 2 cups flour, no cornmeal and add 1 1/2 cup chocolate chips.
