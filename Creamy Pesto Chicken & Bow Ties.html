Creamy Pesto Chicken & Bow Ties

★★★★★

Chicken, Pasta

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 20 min Servings: 4 Source: Campbells.com

Ingredients

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 1/4 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast tenderloins

1 can (10 1/2 ounces) Campbell’s® Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Chicken Soup

1/2 cup basil pesto

1/2 cup milk

1 pound farfalle (bow tie) pasta, cooked and drained

Directions

Season the chicken as desired. Heat the oil in a 12-inch skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until well browned, stirring often.

Stir the soup, pesto sauce and milk in the skillet and heat to a boil. Reduce the heat to low. Cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Stir in the cooked farfalle and cook until the mixture is hot. Season to taste before serving.
