Homemade English Muffins

Breakfast

Ingredients

1 1/4 cups warm water

3 tsp honey

1 1/4 tsp active dry yeast

2 tbsp melted butter

3 1/2 cups all purpose flour

1 tsp salt

cornmeal

Description

English muffin recipe from Facebook

Directions

In medium bowl, add water, honey, and yeast. Set aside for 10 minutes.

Add butter, salt, and flour to the yeast mixture. Mix well. Transfer to a floured surface. Knead dough for 5 mins, adding flour a little at a time until you no longer have sticky dough. Transfer dough into bowl, cover and let rise for 1 hour.

Divide dough into 10 pieces. Form dough into balls. Sprinkle 1tbs cornmeal onto pan lined with parchment paper. Place dough balls on top of cornmeal and flatten into discs to 3/4 inch thick. Lightly spray top of discs and sprinkle on cornmeal. Cover and let rise for 20 mins.

Heat large frying pan, griddle or cast iron skillet to Medium-Low heat and spray with oil. Transfer muffins to pan. Cook for 9 mins until light golden brown and toasted. Flip to 2nd side, and cook for 5 mins until golden and toasted. Enjoy!
