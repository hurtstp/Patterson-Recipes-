Taco Salad

★★★★★

Beef, Salad

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 14oz can kidney beans, drained and rinsed

1 packet taco seasoning

Lettuce

Cherry tomatoes, sliced in half

1 14oz can Black Olives, slice in half

1 English cucumber, large diced

1 small onion, diced

8 oz. Monterey Jack Cheese, grated

Corn chips, about 1/2 of a bag, roughly broken apart (the original recipe calls for Doritos but I use plain corn chips or lime flavored chips)

Thousand Island dressing, about 1 small bottle

Directions

Cook ground beef until brown. Drain excess fat if needed. Add beans and taco seasoning to skillet. Add water according to seasoning packet instructions. Simmer until water is incorporated into beef and beans. Keep warm.

Add chopped lettuce to large bowl. Add tomatoes, olives, cucumber, onion, cheese and chips. Drizzle dressing over salad. Mix to coat and add more dressing if needed.

Serve salad with beef mixture on the side along with chopped avocados.
