Caramelized Onion Pasta with Chilli Oil

Servings: 2 servings Source: Amateurprochef.com

Ingredients

2 servings spaghetti

2 tbsp chilli oil

1/2 cup cream

1 large red onion

3 tbsp olive oil

3-4 cloves of garlic

1 tbsp italian seasoning

salt to taste (~1-2 tsp)

optionally parmesan and chopped parsley

Directions

Start by cutting a red onion into thin slices.

Herat up water, once boiling add in 2 tsp salt and spaghetti (can use any pasta of choice). Cook according to the package instructions.

In a separate pan, add olive, heat up on medium heat for a few minutes until the oil is nice and hot (not smoking) and add in the onions and caramelize on medium heat (roughly 6-7 minutes).

Once caramelized add in chopped garlic cloves, chilli oil and cream.

Add 1 tbsp italian seasoning, salt to taste and optionally add in parmesan and chopped parsely.

Now mix it all up and its time to enjoy!
