Instant Pot Sausage Curls

★★★★★

Instant Pot, Pasta, Pork

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 6 minutes Servings: Yield: 6 Source: Pressureluckcooking.com

Ingredients

1 onion, diced

1 pound Italian sausage, broken into 1/4-inch chunks

4 cloves garlic, minced or pressed

3 cups marinara sauce (1-24 oz jar), divided (I like Rao's or Victoria)

3 1/2 cups chicken broth

1/2 cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves or basil in a tube (about a tablespoon)

1 pound cavatappi or cellentani

1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, with their juices

5 ounces baby spinach

1/2 cup half and half

1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, plus more for topping

4 ounces of a brick of cream cheese

Directions

In large skillet or pot of instant pot add the Add onion, sausage and garlic and sauté until the sausage is just lightly browned (it should not be fully cooked at all).

Add to instant pot. Add 1 1/2 cups of the marinara sauce, broth and fresh basil leaves. Stir until well combined.

Top with the pasta but do not stir. Just smooth it out with a mixing spoon or spatula so it's mostly submerged in the sauce. Top with the can of diced tomatoes.

Secure the lid, move the valve to the sealing position and hit Cancel followed by Manual or Pressure Cook on High Pressure for 6 minutes. Quick release when done.

Add the remaining 1 1/2 cup of marinara, half and half, Parmesan, cream cheese and spinach. Stir until the dairy is fully combined into sauce and spinach is wilted. Let it rest for about 3 minutes to come together before serving topped with additional Parmesan, if desired.
