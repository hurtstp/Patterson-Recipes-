Grilled Chicken Thighs

★★★★★

Chicken

Servings: 6 servings Source: Epicurious.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup soy sauce

1/3 cup (packed) dark brown sugar

2 tablespoons chopped peeled fresh ginger

4 large garlic cloves, chopped

12 skinless boneless chicken thighs (about 3 pounds)

1 teaspoon of sesame oil

Directions

Whisk soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, sesame oil and garlic in large bowl. Remove some of the fat from edges of thighs. Add trimmed chicken thighs to marinade and let sit in the fridge for up to 2 hours.

Grill chicken until cooked through, about 5 minutes per side.
