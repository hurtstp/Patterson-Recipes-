Mom's Lasagna

★★★★★

Pasta, Pork

Source: My Brain

Ingredients

1 box Lasagna noodles

Pasta Sauce - Homemade is best but you can use jarred sauce. You are going to need at least 2- 32 ounce jars. Maybe more.

2 cups mozzerella cheese, shredded.

2 cups parmesan cheese, shredded

1 32 ounce container part skim ricotta cheese

1 box frozen spinach, chopped (defrost in a colander and squeeze out as much water as possible.)

1 egg

salt and pepper to taste

1 pound Italian sausage (Emma - you can leave this out if you are so inclined!)


Description

Best Lasagna in the world!!!

Directions

Boil noodles for 5 minutes. Drain. They will only be partially cooked. Don't worry. They will cook the rest in the oven.

While noodles are cooking, break up into pieces and cook sausage.

In a large bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups mozzerella, 1 1/2 cups parmesan, ricotta, egg and spinach. Salt and pepper to taste.

Preheat over to 375 degrees.

In a 9x13 casserole dish, coat bottom with pasta sauce. Add, 4 lasagna noodles. They may overlap a bit. Spread noodles with a layer of cheese mixture. Sprinkle sausage over top. Cover with sauce. Add 4 lasagna noodles, then cheese, sausage and sauce again. Repeat 2 more times ending with the sauce. Sprinkle remaining cheese over top.

Cover with foil (non stick kind if you have it). Put in oven and cook for 35 minutes. Remove foil and cook for another 15 minutes. Take out of oven and let sit for 10 minutes. It will be easier to cut if its had time to rest.

Take a bite and be reminded that this is the best lasagna in the world!

Notes

You will not use all the cooked noodles. I throw them away but if you are clever, you can figure out something to do with them.
