Hummus in Pita

Sandwiches & Quesadilas & Burgers & Pizza, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 10 mins Servings: Serves: 1 Source: Myrecipes.com

Ingredients

1/4 cup diced cucumber, peeled if desired

1/4 cup diced tomato

1/4 cup diced red onion

1/4 cup chopped black olives

1/4 cup crumbled feta

1/2 teaspoon olive oil

1/2 teaspoon red wine vinegar

Salt and pepper 1/2 cup Classic Hummus 1 6-inch whole-wheat pita, cut in half and gently opened

Directions

In a small bowl, toss vegetables and cheese with oil, vinegar and a pinch each of salt and pepper. Spread hummus evenly in pita halves and fill halves with vegetable mixture.
