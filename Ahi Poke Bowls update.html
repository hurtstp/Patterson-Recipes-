Ahi Poke Bowls

Prep Time: 0 Cook Time: 0 Servings: 4 servings Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

60 ml low-sodium soy sauce

2 tsp. rice vinegar

2 tsp. sesame oil

1 tsp. freshly grated ginger

Pinch of crushed chilli flakes

2 spring onions, thinly sliced, plus more for garnish

1 tsp. toasted sesame seeds, plus more for garnish

450 g sushi-grade ahi tuna, cut into bite-size pieces

FOR SERVING

Cooked white or brown rice

Sliced avocado

Sliced cucumber

Edamame

Shredded carrots

Sliced radish

Directions

In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, chilli flakes, spring onions, and sesame seeds. Add tuna and toss to coat. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes or up to 1 hour.

To serve, add rice to the bottom of four bowls. Top with tuna and toppings of your choice. Garnish with spring onions and sesame seeds before serving.
