BBQ Ribs Rub

Seasonings, Sauces and Spices

Prep Time: 5 min Cook Time: preheating your air fryer is an important step! you'll want to preheat at 400degf for 5 minutes. then, when it's ready, cook the pork chops in the air fryer for 12 minutes, flipping them over after 6 minutes. check for doneness with an instant-read food thermometer. we're aiming for an internal temp of 140degf. if your pork chops are very thick, they may require an additional few minutes. Servings: Yield: 2 Source: Recipeteacher.com

Ingredients

2 tablespoons brown sugar

1 tablespoon paprika

1 ½ teaspoons salt

1 ½ teaspoons fresh ground black pepper

1 teaspoon ground mustard

½ teaspoon onion powder

¼ teaspoon garlic powder
